## Rules:
Flock/Swarm:
  - avoid collisions between boids
  - match velocity of surrounding boids
  - fly towards the center of mass of surrounding boids
  - avoid the predator
 
 Predator:
  - fly towards the center of mass of surrounding boids (more vision than the Flock)
